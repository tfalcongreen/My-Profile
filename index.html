Project Idea

Internet Service Consulting

Overview

For consumers today there are many options for Internet delivery types, Service Providers and hardware. My project is to take the guesswork out of the consumer's hands and streamline the connection process. Stable internet access is no longer a luxury, it has become one of the most necessary tools for laymen and internet professionals alike. ISP's provide some of the tools to figure out the right internet service, but none confidently compare the connection right to the person's premises. This project aims, through the use of existing and new technology, to connect the user to the most stable, cost effective and fastest internet provider that is available to them.

Motivation

With over 400,000 new NBN connections alone since the beginning of 2020link Australians are more connected than ever, but how many of these customers are actually happy with their service? A survey by Choice.com.au, showed that 53% of the respondents faced issues with their service in the 6 months prior to the study. With embarrasing figures such as these, consumers need more information and assistance finding the proper connection. Using a Raspberry Pi with connection to each of the service providers, a technician could assist a customer to test each of the options, as well as find the best placement for the modem inside the premesis. No longer will consumers be forced to rely on empty speed guarantee's from ISP's and prices they shouldn't have to pay.

Description

This project will rely on a number of technologies to test and compare connections and a user interface for consumers to access the service through. After recieveing a prototype, custom printed Raspberry Pi with multiple Sim slots,modems, an LCD interface and an antenna fitted, the process of setting up the software would commence. The Pi will be programmed to read out the names of each different ISP and a "speed-test" style health meter. The Technican will move from room to room in the house or business, to download a small packet of information from each of the providers. These results will read in real time on the screen for transparency with customer, as well as being transferred to a server to hold the information for later use and comparison. A full breakdown of the results will also be sent to the customer with detailed information as well a easy to read "colour map" of the premesis, a zoned grid of the floor plan to simply define the places in which the connection is most stable. Once the customer is happy with a suitable ISP, they can use the Project's interface to access their desired plan. The Project's interface will be a simple html format website for customers to simply book an appointment with a technician. Through the website they will also be able to view the history of testing within the Project, which will be updated in real time as more and more consultations occur. The website will be managed using private servers as the blueprint's of houses may be liable to attack from criminals. Hosting the servers within the company protects the information gathered as cyber-attacks are becoming more common. The servers will be encrypted with the highest possible security. While the Project is aimed at the individual consumer, the service will be available to real estate offices to offer a blueprint of the connectivity of the premesis. This is especially important for tech oriented businesses to aid them in choosing the right lease for their internet needs. The product is highly scalable without the need for the technology to change. Whether for Individuals or Businesses, this Product is highly desireable for consumers to quickly and conscientiously choose an internet service, which in a tech driven world, is becoming more of a necessity.

Tools and Technologies

-

The tools required to complete this project are a mix of hardware and software, each element will be proprietary in nature and custom made for the service. The hardware needed will be a custom printed Raspberry Pi with several modems and Sim-slots capable of connecting to multiple service providers, both broadband, 4g and 5g, simultaneously. An antennae will be fixed to measure the signals from each room and an LCD screen will be fitted to return the results to the operating technician. Software required will consist of basic website development tools such as HTML, CSS, JS. They will be compiled in a repository at Github.com. The "Colour-map" will be created with a combination of the Adobe suite tools like PS, AI and other 3d modeling tools such as Blender.

Outcome

If this project is successful, consumers will have choice to attain a stronger connection to the internet as well as the world around them. This project will not only aid individuals in gaining better networks but also businesses as well. through the implementation of the technologies mentioned, finally there will be no excuse for a poor connection.
